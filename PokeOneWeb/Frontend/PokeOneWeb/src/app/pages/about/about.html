Hi and welcome to the Unofficial PokéOne Guide!

This website is a personal project of me - FinrodAmandil - and attempts to help you as PokéOne player to quickly look up any information you might need about the game. This website was born out of the spreadsheet-based Unofficial PokéOne Guide, which was created all the way back in 2018 as a community effort. The spreadsheet still exists (Link) and is still maintained. Some of the guides and data is only available in the spreadsheet version, while other data is exclusive to this website. Data that is present in both places (mainly spawns and items) I try to keep in sync.

If you find any bugs, wrong or missing data points, have a really cool idea for a new feature or a quality of life improvement, or want to let me know how much you dislike the design of the site, please do tell me about it! Find me on the official PokéOne Discord (username Finrod_Amandil) or in PokéOne (usernames FinrodAmandil, Finrude, Finrod, Findarato)
